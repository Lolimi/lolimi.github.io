About Chunk Hoppers

Chunk Hoppers was the original plugin out of which RCHoppers was born. At the time i had just started out as a programmer and wanted to try out what i could do on this huge project. Since then the plugin evolved with me getting better at optimizing and finding nicer methods to do certain things.

The beauty of Chunk Hoppers is that it doesn't in any way change the vanilla gameplay, but instead it adds practically a new block and the best part about it is, that it doesn't only help on the servers performance, but it is also fun to play with and useful, especially on SkyBlock like servers!
